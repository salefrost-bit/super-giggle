15 · HOW TO PLAY

How to Play
7
The deck is your trainer. Every card is an exercise — the number is your rep count. Do the work, tap the card, next one's dealt.
▾
A standard deck holds 52 cards and 2 jokers. Suits are muscle groups: ♥ push, ♣ pull, ♠ legs, ♦ core. The Ace is 1 — the lightest card — and the royals carry J=11, Q=12, K=13. The deck runs empty, the session ends — time and points go straight to your history and XP.
